Our Services

We offer complete design and engineering services for digital signage support systems and metal fittings. Our workflow includes:

  2. Concept Planning – Functional specs, patent checks, feasibility study.
  3. Concept Design – Structural layout, component layout, production methods.
  4. Detailed Design – Assembly-focused drawings, QCD objectives, tool selections.
  5. Electrical Design – Control panel design, ladder sequence, operation board.
