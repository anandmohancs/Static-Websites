Animals are on the move like never before at viserlife Zoo with Zoo360, a first-in-the-world system of see-through trails passing through treetops, crossing over pathways and connecting habitats, giving animals like amazing big cats, majestic primates and marvelous meerkats the opportunities to travel and explore. Visit western lowland gorillas, giraffes, hippos, white rhino, zebras, red pandas, Amur tigers and more at America’s first Zoo.

For decades, the Zoo has played an integral role in conservation efforts for one of the world’s most threatened primates, the golden lion tamarin (GLT), a small charismatic monkey found only in the forests of Brazil.
